Must see places in Salt Lake City

Start your outdoor activities at The Great Salt Lake, the largest saltwater river in the western hemisphere. Enjoy the river by hiking or biking.

Salt Lake City has a large number of breweries brewed in the region. Be sure you will enjoy some of the best beers in the United States, this activity is reserved for visitors over 21 years old.

If you are visiting the city to practice winter sports, you are in the right place. Salt Lake City is one of the best places to ski or snowboard in the United States, we recommend you buy a pass that will make you forget about the lines.

Best time to visit

The best time to visit the city is undoubtedly in early autumn, when temperatures drop, but are still pleasant, during this season the city is full of concerts and events to say goodbye to summer.
